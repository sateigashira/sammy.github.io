Founder/Director UCD Club/Server Directory

when the pandemic hit, we were all devastated, and many suffered. One common factor was loneliness casued by the self afflicting social isolation we needed to stay safe. Students had trouble networking, finding clubs and communities they could connect with to help heal their mental health. I knew discord was widely used by many students, especially those in the gaming/esports communities. And that's how I created the Directory. This is a discord server that has helped to unite the UC Davis community and provide security in chaos.

This Directory provides students with general information and discord server links for clubs, student made communities, dorm halls, course servers, university resources and programs, and access to announcements from university staff, clubs, and communities. The Directory servers as a resource for all students and is continually growing and developing. We are now at 11.5k students. Some initiatives in progress include:

  • Scholarships
  • Creating Student Jobs
  • Student Community and Club support funds
  • Emergency relief support
