About Me

Samuel Petruescu

Hello! My name is Samuel and I am a recent UC Davis graduate. I graduated in June of 2022 with a BS in Applied Physics and an emphasis in Physical Electronics. I will be pursuing a Phd/Masters degree in Applied Physics/EE focusing on Quantum Computing as my area of research. This will help prepare me to Enter and contribute to the quantum computing industry and help change the world hopefully, one qubit at a time.

Outside of my studies, I am very passionate about community developement and supporting my fellow peers as well as serving at my local church. A few of my hobbies include gaming/esports, writing fiction, backpacking, and airsoft. I was born and raised in San Jose California but my parents immigrated from Romania. I'm a lemonade connoisuer and I also enjoy watching anime in my free time. Please don't hesitate to shoot me a message if you'd like to talk about anything!
