Sonshine Ministries Volunteer

During the summer of 2018, I spent 6 weeks up on Shasta lake serving as a Barnabas Team Member (Barney) of Sonshine Specialized Camping Ministries (SSCM). It was truly an amazing experience in which I got to learn a lot, and serve a lot of students as both mentor and friend. SSCM is a ministry in which 2 ministry staff are assigned to one houseboat, and are in charge of managing and supporting activities for churches who come and live on those houseboats for a week at a time. The majority of students who we served on these houseboats were middle school to high school students. If churches brought enough students, some houseboats would get tied together in a "tie" which means up to 100 campers in a single spot. Some of my duties included:

  • Cooking/keeping fed and cleaning for up to 25 campers, making sure to keep a clean houseboat at any given time
  • Responsible for watching over and keeping all campers safe, including during water based activities
  • Mentoring and emotionally supporting campers in any way possible, providing spiritual guidance where needed
  • Ability to operate for weeks on end with only a few hours of sleep each now is a necessity

For more info see: http://www.sonshineministries.com
